Abu dhabi

Places to Visit:

  • Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque
  • AThe Corniche
  • Emirates Palace
  • Abu Dhabi Desert Tours
  • Jebel Hafeet & The Al Ain Camel Souk
  • Al Jahili Fort


Things to Do:

  • You can spend hours on Yas Island, home to the world's largest indoor theme park
  • Warner Bros. World™ Abu Dhabi
  • the shopper's paradise, Yas Mall.
  • Watch world-class shows at the state-of-the-art Etihad Arena
  • be a part of the world's greatest racing event at the finale of the Formula 1™ season at Yas Marina Circuit.

Local Tips:

  • Do dress the part.
  • Do embrace the sand
  • Don't overlook navigating by landmarks.
  • Don't forget to come prepared for the weather.
  • Do take the time to LEARN about the Emirati culture.

SEOUL

Places to Visit:

  • Gyeongbokgung Palace
  • the war memorial of korea
  • Myeongdong Shopping Street
  • National Museum of Korea
  • N Seoul Tower
  • Insadong
  • Namsan park
  • Cheonggyecheon Stream
  • Bongeunsa Temple
  • Bunchun honak school

Things to Do:

  • try famous foods
  • Plan a day trip to the naturally abundant Nami Island
  • Be mesmerized by the tiles inside the Blue Mosque
  • Take a beautiful stroll through the Cheonggyecheon Stream
  • Wander through the serious beauty of Seoul Forest
  • Unveil the magic world of the Alchemy of Souls
  • Have some fun on a Han River Cruise
  • Spend the day at Seoraksan National Park
  • Marvel at the beauty of the bridge fountain called Banpo Bridge
  • Dive into the world of Vincent van Gogh in Seoul`s 360º digital art showcase.
  • Witness Seoul’s famous Silver Grass at Haneul Park
  • Check out the trendiest cafes in Seoul

Local Tips:

  • have a language translator installed in you phone
  • use subways to save money
  • get a T- money card if your stay is for than two days
  • most stores accept credit card
  • wifi is everywhere but unreliable
  • Street food is clean and safe to upon your arrival
  • difficult to find HALAL food for muslims
  • tourist information sites and your hotel concierge is your bestfriend

ISTANBUL

Places to Visit:

  • Hagia Sophia Mosque
  • Suleymaniye Mosque
  • Sultanahmet District
  • Bosphorus Strait
  • Galata Tower
  • Eyup Sultan Mosque

Things to Do:

  • Marvel at the lofty dome of the Hagia Sophia
  • Wander inside the Topkapı Palace
  • Be mesmerized by the tiles inside the Blue Mosque
  • Travel back in time to Byzantium at the Basilica Cistern
  • Shop for spices and souvenirs at the Spice Bazaar
  • Scarf down a balık ekmek on the go at Eminönü

Local Tips:

  • AVOID VISITING IN SUMMER
  • USE THE HAVABUS (HAVAIST) SHUTTLE TO TRAVEL TO/FROM THE AIRPORT
  • THERE ARE MULTIPLE AIRPORTS IN ISTANBUL – DON’T FRONT UP AT THE WRONG ONE!
  • CARD IS WIDELY ACCEPTED, BUT IT HELPS TO CARRY CASH
  • MOST ATMS IN TURKEY CHARGE A FEE
  • THE PUBLIC TRANSPORT SYSTEM IS PHENOMENAL
  • PHARMACIES ARE HARD TO FIND
  • EMBRACE CAT CULTURE
