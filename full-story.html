Destination


Switzerland

Places to Visit:

  • Zürich
  • Geneva
  • Zermatt
  • Basel
  • Lucerne
  • Bern
  • interlaken

Things to Do:

  • Stroll a meticulously preserved medieval town
  • Meet Charlie Chaplin in Lausanne
  • Take a tuk-tuk tour in Geneva
  • Explore the Swiss-Italian region
  • Eat your way through central Switzerland


Local Tips:

  • Pack for all weathers.
  • Download the SBB app and look for Supersaver tickets
  • Be prepared to splash the cash
  • Don’t assume everyone is multilingual
  • Sprechen Sie Deutsch? Swiss German is not the same
  • Don’t buy bottled water – tap water is just as good
  • Stay safe in the mountains – and watch out for cows
